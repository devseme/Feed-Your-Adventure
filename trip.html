Accommodation

Sabyinyo Silverback Lodge

Luxury Accommodation in Volcanoes National Park

Set close to the park headquarters in the foothills of the volcanoes, Sabyinyo's private cottages are set in expansive gardens with magnificent views. Relax by the fireside or head up to the main lodge with bar, restaurant, library and games room.

Ruzizi Tented Lodge

Classic Accommodation in Akagera National Park

A small but charming and authentic lodge overlooking Lake Ihema. It’s an ideal base from which to tour Akagera but also to simply stay and enjoy a sundowner and watch hippos in the lake.

Nyungwe House

Luxury Accommodation in Nyungwe National Park

The first One & Only hotel in Rwanda and set in a tea plantation on the edge of Nyungwe Park. It’s easy to visit waterfalls, chimpanzee trek or try the exhilarating jungle canopy walk.

The Retreat

Luxury Accommodation in Kigali

The Retreat is a 20-room luxury boutique hotel created by the American owners of Heaven. The Retreat is Kigali’s first eco-friendly resort featuring solar electricity and locally-crafted furniture from a Tanzanian sustainable teak farm and all organic amenities. The Retreat offers a solar heated saltwater swimming pool with diving rocks and a hot tub, a luxurious open-air gym and spa, a yoga and fitness deck, conference facilities, a spacious living room, and breakfast at Fusion Restaurant. The Retreat’s new wing has 8 Luxury Pool Villas, each with a private saltwater heated plunge pool, outdoor showers, and 120 square meters of private indoor and outdoor space. The Superior King Suites feature front and rear private patios, double sinks with teak vanities, and indoor and outdoor rain showers.
