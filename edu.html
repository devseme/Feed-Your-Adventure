Study in Rwanda

Did You Know?

Rwanda occupies position 75 and 121 in the world economy

and world education ranking respectively!Rwanda has plenty

to offer students, particularly the interesting

Rwandese culture.

01

Bachelor

Rwanda offers 133 bachelor programs in 14 universities. There are two main types of bachelor’s degrees in standard academic disciplines in Rwanda: three-year ordinary degrees (360 credits) and four-year honors degrees (480 credits).

02

Masters

Master’s programs are between one and two years in length. Admission requirements include an honors bachelor’s degree with high grades (second-class honors, upper division), as well as good English skills.

03

PHD

If you want further education beyond the undergraduate level or if you want more personal development or a career in academia, you could obtain a PhD degree. PhD degrees are programmes that usually follow a Master's.
